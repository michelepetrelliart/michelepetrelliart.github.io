Each work in this gallery represents the definitive transition from digital vision to material form. These are not serial reproductions, but Unique Physical Realizations 1/1: once the object is materialized, the digital source is officially archived, ensuring that no duplicate exists within the physical realm.

The presentation utilizes a custom 3.5" (9cm) Museum Acid-Free Passepartout in Anthracite, featuring a precise white 45° bevel cut. This architectural framing, housed within a 50mm Solid Wood Showcase Frame, transforms the artwork into a three-dimensional sculptural object.

Protected by industry-leading Schott Amiran® Museum Glass and printed on high-definition Hahnemühle German Etching 310gsm paper, the artwork is shielded from UV radiation while retaining absolute structural and chromatic integrity.

Note: The main image represents a concept of a custom sculptural installation. Standard realization is provided in the 50mm Solid Wood Showcase Frame as specified.
