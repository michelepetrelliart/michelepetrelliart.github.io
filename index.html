MICHELE PETRELLI
There is no one here. There is nothing to elevate, nothing to heal.
The truth is implacable. Everything that happens is governed by blind necessity.
The Universe is not benevolent or hostile: It is neutral. What manifests must dissolve.
This is not nihilism. We are the Field: amoral Consciousness.
Art does not heal. It holds up the implacable mirror of our illusion.
