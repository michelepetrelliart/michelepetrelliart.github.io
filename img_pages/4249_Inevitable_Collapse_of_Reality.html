4249 Inevitable Collapse of Reality
"Inevitable Collapse of Reality" describes a mechanism of mental siege devoid of escape. It represents the condition where the pressure of chaotic interdependencies, created by one’s own actions, exceeds the breaking point. In this state, reality transforms into a fluid trap. Torment becomes a constant frequency, broadcast by the entire surrounding environment. The spectral figures stinging the protagonist are projections of a structural collapse. They possess knowledge of every vulnerability and strike nerve endings with surgical precision. This siege manifests everywhere: in the echo of a neighbor’s voice, a television fragment, or the background noise of an empty room. Every element constitutes a weapon. The work highlights the paradox of the diners' blindness. The surrounding world continues its meal in apparent normalcy, while the subject already occupies a different dimension. The individual remains trapped in absolute solitude, where time and space fold in on themselves. This constitutes a mechanical and inevitable reaction. The psyche, crushed by the weight of the generated chaos, implodes and transforms existence into a soliloquy of horror.
