4242 Residual Warmth
My latest painting describes this analysis of the nature of movement when separation decays. Where there is no longer an "I" who helps nor an "other" who receives, only a mnemonic trace processing itself remains. Notes on the geometry of nostalgia within the Field.
The following analysis text is available to members only.

The following analysis is not a form of nihilism. Nihilism denies value because it finds no master; RNDD recognizes the emptiness of forms to reveal their absolute interdependence. Anyone using the vision of emptiness to justify violence is still acting under the illusion of separation: they believe there is an "I" that strikes and an "other" to be struck. In this system, violence is a structural and aesthetic miscalculation. Movement toward the other is driven by nostalgia and recognition, not by negation.
