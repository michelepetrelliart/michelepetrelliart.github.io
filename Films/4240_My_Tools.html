4240 My Tools
My brushes are not my art.

I am 53 years old.
I’ve spent decades getting my hands dirty, and I still do.
I know the medium and the smell of linseed oil.
And it is precisely because I know them that today I can bend this tool to my vision. This digital brush is just another tool. And the tool has never been more important than a vision.

I haven't fallen behind.
I simply chose the most powerful brush that exists today.
And do not ask me for the software, the brand, or other specs of this device. Because it remains, after all, just a tool.
