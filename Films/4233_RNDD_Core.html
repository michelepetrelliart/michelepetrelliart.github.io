This work was made possible through the support of my Patreon members, where it was first released as an integral preview and where our in-depth dialogue continues.
I am publishing this video here, on my personal domain, to preserve the integrity and digital longevity of the RNDD research. Does free will exist? And who is asking the question? Starting from my oil painting process, I explore the concept of RNDD (Non-Dual Radical Determinism). Painting is not a choice, but an inevitable unfolding of reality.
