I am publishing this video here, on my personal domain, to preserve the integrity and digital longevity of the RNDD research.

Does free will exist? And who is asking the question? Starting from my oil painting process, I explore the concept of RNDD (Non-Dual Radical Determinism). Painting is not a choice, but an inevitable unfolding of reality.
