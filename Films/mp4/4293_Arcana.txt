These are 42 of my paintings transformed into personal cards: 22 arcana and 20 extensions.
<br/>
They are not cards for divination, <br/>
but an observation system. <br/><br/>

They serve to map the tangle of processes in which we are immersed, to observe them from the outside and detach ourselves from them. <br/><br/>

The goal is not to dominate reality with the ego, but to recognize the structure in order to stop being its prey.
