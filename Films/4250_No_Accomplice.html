4250 No Accomplice
There is a razor-thin line between those who appreciate dark aesthetics for their philosophical or artistic depth, and those who use them as a mirror for their own frustration or malice. This latter category is not looking for art; they are looking for an accomplice. They seek someone to validate their destructive and cynical view of the world. That someone is best sought before a mirror, in complete solitude.
